Why Start in Australia?

Australia faces slow growth, high household debt, and productivity challenges – but is also advanced in technology and institutions. That makes it the perfect testbed for CEA.

Key Challenges Addressed:
  • Housing stress & affordability
  • Weak productivity & investment
  • Waste and resource misallocation
What CEA Does

AI + Circular Marketplace

CEA combines economic analysis with a circular marketplace. It:

  • Analyses data from government and businesses.
  • Recommends circular solutions (reuse, repair, share).
  • Connects buyers and sellers of surplus and recycled materials.
  • Helps plan infrastructure, housing, and social services.
Who Benefits

Governments, CEOs & Society

CEA is designed for:

  • Government – smarter budgets, less waste, better targeting.
  • Businesses – cheaper inputs, new revenue from surplus stock.
  • Citizens – better housing, services, and long‑term stability.
